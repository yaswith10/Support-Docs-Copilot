# Project Chimera

Project Chimera is an experimental research initiative founded in 2026 to study artificial floating islands in the Indian Ocean.

## Mission

The project's mission is to develop self-sustaining floating cities powered entirely by bioluminescent algae reactors.

## Reactor

The reactor used in Project Chimera is called the Helios-7 Reactor.

The Helios-7 Reactor produces approximately 12.4 gigawatts of energy while consuming only seawater and algae biomass.

## Security Levels

Project Chimera defines four security levels:

- White
- Amber
- Crimson
- Obsidian

Only engineers with Obsidian clearance are allowed to access the reactor control room.

## AI Assistant

The project's AI assistant is named ORION.

ORION always responds using exactly three sentences unless emergency mode is activated.

## Emergency Protocol

Emergency protocol Delta-9 should be activated if the reactor temperature exceeds 913°C.

When Delta-9 is activated:

1. Shut down the algae feed system.
2. Deploy six cooling drones.
3. Disconnect satellite communications.
4. Notify the Arctic Control Center.

## Locations

Project Chimera currently operates three floating research stations:

- Atlas Station
- Horizon Station
- Echo Station

The primary research station is Horizon Station.

## Equipment

Every engineer receives the following equipment:

- Blue access card
- Waterproof tablet
- Helios Toolkit
- Oxygen wristband

## Fun Fact

Every Friday at exactly 16:45 UTC, ORION tells the engineering team a random joke before generating the weekly maintenance report.
